Point your camera at the groceries. Forager does the rest.

Forager is the companion service for Pantry Raider. Snap a photo of your groceries or a receipt and Forager reads it for you: no accounts with AI companies, no keys to paste in, nothing to configure.

How it works

Create a Forager account here, then open the Pantry Raider app in your kitchen and sign in with the same email and password. That is the whole setup: scanning starts working right away.

Every account starts on a free trial. Your inventory, recipes, and shopping lists stay in your own kitchen; photos pass through Forager only long enough to be read, and are never stored.
